Criminal law: A competent authority (in charge of enforcing a law) accuses a person or corporation or other governmental authority of violating state laws and / or bylaws and / or regulations.  Civil Law: Includes quite a few industries and the most prominent of which are contract law, tort law, labor law, family law, property law, corporate law and private international law. The parties to civil law are plaintiff and defendant (including corporations as well as bodies and governmental authorities operating in the civil field, such as contract and tort matters).Administrative Law: Administrative law regulates the actions of the governing authorities acting by virtue of law. A person / corporation petitions the court for relief against the governing body.
